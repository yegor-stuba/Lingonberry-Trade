Core Functionality Outline
Based on your requirements, here's how the bot will function:

Initial Setup:

When a user starts the bot, it will guide them through a setup process
Collect trading preferences (style, markets, pairs)
Collect account size and risk parameters
Store this information for future use
Market Analysis:

Continuously monitor selected markets for trading opportunities
Implement SMC and ICT methodologies for analysis
Generate trade suggestions when good opportunities are found
Trade Suggestion:

Present trade suggestions with detailed analysis
Include entry price, stop loss, take profit, risk-reward ratio
Provide reasoning based on SMC/ICT principles
Include chart visualization
Ask for confirmation (yes/no)
Trade Journaling:

Record trade details when accepted
Update trade status at end of day (00:00 GMT+1)
Calculate performance metrics
Generate statistics and visualizations
Backtesting:

Allow testing of strategies on historical data
Generate performance reports
